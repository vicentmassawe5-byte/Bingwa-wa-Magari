About Bingwa Wa Magari

Tanzania's most trusted name in premium used cars

Our Story

Founded over a decade ago, Bingwa Wa Magari Used has grown from a small family business into one of Tanzania's most respected used car dealerships. Our name "Bingwa" means "Champion" — and that's exactly what we strive to be: champions of quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous 150-point inspection process. We believe our customers deserve nothing less than complete confidence when purchasing a used vehicle.

Located in the heart of Kinondoni, Dar es Salaam, our showroom is open six days a week, welcoming customers from across Tanzania.

Our Process

How We Ensure Quality

01

Sourcing

We carefully select vehicles from trusted sources, prioritizing low mileage, single-owner, and well-maintained cars.

02

Our certified technicians perform a comprehensive 150-point inspection covering engine, transmission, brakes, and electronics.

03

Pricing

We research the market to ensure fair, competitive pricing with no hidden fees or surprises.
